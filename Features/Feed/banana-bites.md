> **ğŸŒ Theta Decay: The Silent Killer**
>
> Theta = how much your option loses value each day just from time passing.
>
> **Example:** Buy a $5 call. Theta is -$0.10. Tomorrow, it's worth $4.90 even if stock doesn't move.
>
> **The closer to expiration, the faster theta kills your option.**
>
> **How to avoid:** Don't hold options too long. Take profits. Don't buy options expiring in <7 days unless you know what you're doing.
>
> **Remember:** Time is NOT on your side with options. â°
