### Account Settings

**Personal Info:**

- Password (change)

- Change anytime

**Preferences:**
- Theme (Light/Dark mode)

### Notification Settings

**What you can control:**
- **Portfolio Alerts** - Price movements, holdings
- **News Alerts** - Breaking news for your stocks
- **Daily Summary** - Morning/evening notifications
- **Trade Confirmations** - Order fills
- **Marketing** - Product updates (opt out anytime)

**Channels:**
- Push notifications (mobile)
- Email
- SMS (optional)

**Control your data:**
- **Portfolio Privacy** - Hide $ amounts (show % only)
- **Activity Privacy** - Share trades with community (opt-in)
- **Data Sharing** - Marketing preferences

**Delete Account:**
- Permanently delete (can't be undone)
