## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### 1. Use Variables
Write `[TICKER]` in your prompt, then fill it in when you use it:
- "Analyze [TICKER] for swing entry"
- "Compare [TICKER1] vs [TICKER2]"
- "What's the risk in [TICKER]?"

When you tap the prompt, AI will ask you which ticker!

### 2. Build a Daily Routine
Create prompts for your daily workflow:
1. "Morning Market Check" (6:30 AM)
2. "Find Today's Setups" (9:00 AM)
3. "Mid-Day Momentum" (12:00 PM)
4. "End of Day Review" (4:30 PM)

Tap each at the right time = instant routine.

### 3. Organize by Timeframe
Name prompts clearly:
- `[DAILY] Morning Movers`
- `[WEEKLY] Swing Setups`
- `[MONTHLY] Portfolio Review`

Easy to find what you need!

### 4. Copy Great Questions
When AI gives amazing analysis, **save that question** as a custom prompt. Don't lose good questions!

### 5. Iterate and Improve
First version doesn't have to be perfect:
- Use it a few times
- See what's missing
- Edit and improve
- Repeat

Your prompts get better over time!
