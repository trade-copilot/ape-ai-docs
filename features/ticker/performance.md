## ğŸ’¡ How to Use Performance

### For Day Traders (Blitz)
- Focus on 1D and 5D charts
- Watch intraday levels
- Volume is key
- Quick scalps on bounces/breakouts

### For Swing Traders (Maverick)
- Focus on 1M and 3M charts
- Identify swing setups
- Entry at support, exit at resistance
- Hold 3-10 days

### For Long-Term (Sage)
- Focus on 1Y, 5Y, MAX charts
- Ignore day-to-day noise
- Buy dips in uptrends
- Hold for years
